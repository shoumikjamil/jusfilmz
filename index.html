THE COMPANY FOUNDERS

It all began with a small spark in interest in making videos and films. JUS Filmz was created in 2012 by three friends with a passion in movie making. Those three people are Jasper, Uzair, and Shoumik thus creating the acronym of JUS. The main goal of JUS was to create movies and films but JUS continues to go into many different fields of multimedia and production.
